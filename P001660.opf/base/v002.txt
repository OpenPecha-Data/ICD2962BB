22) Byang chub kyi sems kyi don bsgom pa'i "yi ge gzhi la'm 'bras bu’id !
gsal ba;
This is the second and concluding volume of Bdud-'joms Rin-po-che's
incomplete manuscript of the collected works of the famed Sog-bzlog-pa Blo-gros-
rgyal-mtshan (b. 1552).
The present volume is missing a total of 71 titles, most of them quite
small but some of great importance. It is hoped that spese day some or all of
these may be located.

volume is missing the following texts :
